Pre-Requirements

  • A full-time student enrolled in an AA, AS, or BA program (not AAS) who has not yet completed a bachelor’s degree.
  • 60 credits completed at end of current semester at a college or university in the US.
  • Exceptional students with 50+ credits may be considered; contact the program director before submitting an application.
  • 3.5+ GPA.
  • Completion of freshman and sophomore level prerequisites in the intended major (2-4 courses as appropriate).
  • Coursework in all of the following general education areas: composition, humanities, social sciences, math, laboratory science.

THP Academic Requirements

Maintain a 3.3 GPA

Complete 12 credits hours per semester

  • An honors minor related to the are of your major: Humanities, Math & Natural Sciences or Social Sciences.
  • BALA (Business and Liberal Arts a minor in Business-related coursework ; it is designed to complement a Humanities or Social Sciences major
  • Departmental Honors are available in English, Math, Music, Computer Science, Economics, Anthropology.
  • There are honors sequence for majors in Accounting, Communication Sciences and Disorders, Education (SEYS or EECE), Family, Nutrition and Exercise Science, and Neuroscience.
  • Participate in an Accelerated Master's program Click here for more information.
